Places to Stay in Miami Beach

Miami Beach is home to a plethora of hotels, resorts and motels found all along the beach. One of the most famous hotels is the Fontainebleau located at 4441 Collins Ave. Not only is it a hotel, but it is also a spa! Another popular hotel to stay at is the Eden Roc found at 4525 Collins Ave. Collins Ave is the prime location to stay in Miami Beach due to the easy access to the beach. Many of the best hotels to stay at in Miami Beach are found on Collins Ave.
