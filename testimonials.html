Reviews of Miami Beach

Barbera, age 29. "I have lived in Miami Beach for 11 years, and I have never gotten bored. Whether I am spending the entire day at the beach, or walking along Lincoln Road, shopping. During the night, I feel like the city never stops, there is always something to be doing. If I ever get bored, I can take a quick flight to Mexico or the Caribbean due to close proximity."

John, age 45. "I don't even know how long I have lived in Miami for but all I can say is that I absolutely love it here! I think it is such a great atmosphere to raise my kids in. Not only do my kids learn how to have fun, they learn how to be independent and navigate a city."

Amy, age 17. "Growing up on Miami Beach, I would not trade in my experiences for anything. I lived the vibrant night life and the beautiful, accessible beaches which were filled with sunshine. I love that I could just walk down to the beach and do my homework or that during school we could walk down the street and get take out. Miami Beach is the best and I recommend visiting." "
